Have your Speedcoins always with you, in your pocket! You pay by quickly scanning a QR-code. As a merchant, you receive payments reliably and instantly. Speedcoin Wallet is the first mobile Speedcoin app, and arguably also the most secure!

FEATURES:

• No registration, web service or cloud needed! This wallet is de-centralized and peer to peer.
• Display of Speedcoin amount in SPD and mSPD.
• Conversion to and from national currencies.
• Sending and receiving of Speedcoin via NFC, QR-codes or Speedcoin URLs.
• Address book for regularly used Speedcoin addresses.
• When you're offline, you can still pay via Bluetooth.
• System notification for received coins.
• App widget for Speedcoin balance.

You can find more about Speedcoin at
https://www.speedcoin.org/

If you want to contribute to Speedcoin Wallet, the project is located at
https://github.com/spdcoin/Speedcoin-wallet
